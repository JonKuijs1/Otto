The goal

Getting children to go to sleep and keeping them in bed in the morning. So you dont have to worry about your child getting enough sleep.

Visual style

Made with a friendly looking stuffed animal to bring children joy and give them something that they can call a friend.

Why a otter

Otters sleep holding hands to give your kid a safe feeling we made one that your child can sleep hand in hand with

Going to sleep early

The otter will help your child go to sleep early by telling him he's tired an asking if he wants to help him with sleeping.

Out of bed on time

OTTO will try to help stay your child in bed until you want him to come out of bed. This way you can sleep in as well

environment friendly

OTTO is made with recycled to protect the enviorment from pollution. We also would like to convinse you to take care of our enviorment.

Control OTTO by using the app

Set a sleep time and a wake-up time in the app so your child gets to bed in time and doesn’t wake you up in the early mornings.

Simple controls

Controls are designed to make it easy to set a time for your child to sleep or wake up thx to the app this can be done from downstairs while you child is already in bed.

Battery life

You can also see the battery life OTTO has left in the app so you can see when its running low. And make sure OTTO’s battery doesn’t run out.

Set a time for every day

In the settings you can set a sleep and wake up time for every time of the week so you can do it once and don’t have to look back at it again.

How does the charger work?

The charger works threw wireless charging if you put the charger in OTTO’s arms it will automatically start charging.

The charger design

The charger is made as a fish this is done because otters eat fish it feels natural to give OTTO a fish because he likes that.

Fast charging

Thanks to the 7,5-watt charging system OTTO will be on 100% battery life when your child gets back from school, so he never experiences a OTTO without battery life.

Battery life

OTTO’s battery life is around 48 hours but so it can last 2 days without charging but with OTTO build in request to be charged it’s hard to forget to charge him up.

Charging OTTO

OTTO gets charged by giving him his fish when he says he is hungry and wants his fish you child will give him his fish and OTTO will charge automatically.

How OTTO interacts

OTTO interacts with you child by talking to him also because of his light censors it can see when the room is to light. And pics up when your child is moving out of bed thanks to the motion sensor.

HOW DO THE INTERACTIONS WORK

How will OTTO help with sleeping

OTTO helps your child with sleeping by being there friend who needs help with sleeping. Your child will teach OTTO how to sleep but in the process of this your child will fall asleep himself. This way your child will hopefully find fun in going to bed.

HOW DOEST IT HELP

How OTTO interacts

How it works OTTO will pick up light with his censors if there is light, he will ask your child if he will turn of the lights because he can’t sleep with the lights on. OTTO will also say things like: ‘can you hold my hand while trying to sleep I am scared otherwise’ he will ask for help trying to sleep by talking to your child. Also the motion sensors can pick up when your child wakes up and will tell him to stay in bed if he or she is up early. The times OTTO will try to get your child to go to sleep or stay in bed depends on the time the parents set in the OTTO app.

How will OTTO help with sleeping

OTTO will work because it gives you child a reason to go to bed and make it somewhat fun to sleep also, your child is so busy trying to get OTTO to sleep that in the process he will fall asleep himself because OTTO will tell him all kinds of tricks to help him fall asleep. Your child will proceed to give an example to OTTO on how to do it and while doing this he will fall asleep himself. Also in the morning when your child wakes up to early while you still want to rest OTTO will say: “your parents are still sleeping and I am still tired myself shell we stay in bed for another hour?” this way you child will not come running in your room early In the morning.
